Kailvora Infra Private Limited ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates the website and provides real estate services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and utilize our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, and any other information you voluntarily provide through contact forms or consultations.
