About RERA

The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA) is a landmark legislation enacted by the Government of India to regulate the real estate sector and promote transparency and accountability in the industry. Kailvora Infra Private Limited is fully committed to complying with all provisions of RERA.

RERA Compliance Commitment

Kailvora Infra is committed to:

  • Maintaining complete transparency in all real estate transactions
  • Providing accurate project information to buyers and investors
  • Adhering to all timelines and delivery commitments
  • Following all statutory requirements and regulations
  • Protecting buyer interests and maintaining trust
  • Handling complaints and grievances promptly and fairly

Key RERA Provisions We Follow

  • Transparency in Advertising: All project information, specifications, and terms are clearly communicated
  • Proper Documentation: All agreements and contracts comply with RERA standards
  • Escrow Accounts: Buyer funds are maintained in escrow accounts as per RERA requirements
  • Progress Updates: Regular updates on project progress and construction milestones
  • Compliance with Specifications: Projects are developed strictly as per approved plans and specifications
  • Grievance Redressal: Dedicated system for addressing buyer complaints and concerns

Buyer Protection Under RERA

Under RERA, buyers are protected through:

  • Right to full information about the project
  • Protection against misrepresentation by developers
  • Guaranteed interest rate on delayed refunds
  • Compensation for delay in possession
  • Right to lodge complaints with RERA authorities
  • Enhanced security of investments through escrow accounts
